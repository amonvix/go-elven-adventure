# ⚔️ Phase 01 — Village of Awakening

## Quest 2: The Soul of the Hero

In the ancient lands of Eldora, no warrior may begin a journey without identity, strength, and purpose.

The Elder Scribe has summoned you.

> "Young traveler... before you cross the gates of destiny, your essence must be recorded in the Book of Origins.  
> Tell us your name, your current level, and whether life still burns within you.  
> Only then may your adventure continue."

Your mission is to create your hero's first data record using the language of Go.

## Objective:

Learn how variables work in Go and how to store values in memory.

## Concepts Learned

- `var`
- short declaration `:=`
- strings
- integers
- booleans
- printing variables with `fmt.Println()`

## Your Task

Create a new file called:

```text
hero.go
```

# 👹 Mini Boss Fight — The Twin Messengers

The roads of Eldora are under threat.

Two messengers must cross the kingdom carrying news of hope, but the Royal Registry only recognizes warriors with complete records.

The Elder Scribe commands:

> "Create the identity of two heroes and present them to the realm.  
> Only then shall the gates remain open."

## Objective:

Use variables to create **two different hero profiles**.

## Requirements:

Create data for:

### Hero One
- Name
- Class
- Age
- Lost status

### Hero Two
- Name
- Class
- Age
- Lost status

Then print both profiles clearly.

## Expected Example:

Hero: Amon | Class: Apprentice | Age: 41 | Lost: true  
Hero: Elyra | Class: Ranger | Age: 27 | Lost: false

## Rules:

- Use proper types (`string`, `int`, `bool`)
- Use `fmt.Println()`
- Keep the output readable

## Reward Unlocked:

- Multi-variable thinking
- Repetition awareness
- Preparation for Structs in future phases

## Victory Condition:

Both heroes successfully announced to Eldora.
