# ⚔️ Phase 02 — Forest of Decisions

## Quest 2: Necromancer secret

The Guild Hall grows restless.

Reports arrive from every region of Eldora.

Travelers speak of cursed ruins where the same sounds repeat endlessly.
Villagers whisper about magical rituals trapped in infinite cycles.
Even the forest itself seems to echo the same warnings over and over again.

The Guild Master closes his eyes for a moment.

Then, slowly, he speaks:

> "A single action may save a traveler...
> but repeated actions may save a kingdom."

Silence fills the hall.

The old mage strikes his staff against the stone floor.

A pulse of ancient energy spreads through the chamber.

The torches flicker.

And a new word appears in glowing runes before the heroes:

LOOP
